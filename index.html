Cpt. James Tiberius Kirk

The man who made the universe a better place

Here's a time line of Cpt. Kirk`s life:

  • 2233 - Born in Iowa.
  • 2250 - He finishes his studies at the Starfleet Academy with the rank of Cadet.
  • 2254 - Assigned to the starship Farragut with the rank of Lieutenant
  • 2260 - Assigned to the starship Enterprise.
  • 2263 - Appointed captain of the starship USS Enterprise.
  • 2264—2269 - Under Kirk's command, the USS Enterprise completed its historic five-year exploration mission.
  • 2269 - He was assigned to Starfleet Headquarters.
  • 2271 - Admiral Kirk took temporary command of the Enterprise to fight in the Imperial Crisis.
  • 2282 - Return to service after retirement.
  • 2285 - For "stealing" the USS Enterprise and sabotaging the starship USS Excelsior to save his friend Spock, Kirk was demoted to captain and assigned to the new Enterprise NCC-1701-A.
  • 2293 - A Klingon court sentenced Kirk to imprisonment in one of the mining colonies for the murder of the Klingon chancellor Gorkon. However, he was later released, which is Spock's merit, and the charge against him was dropped.
  • 2293 - During the tests of the new Enterprise-V ship, Kirk disappeared (and, as expected, died). In fact, he got into a temporary anomaly, where he met Captain Picard from 2371 and helped him stop Tolian Soran, who decided to destroy 230 million lives.
  • 2293 - Die at the age of 60.
